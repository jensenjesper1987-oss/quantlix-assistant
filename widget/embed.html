Quantlix Assistant Widget

Production embed example. The browser points at your assistant backend, and that backend calls your Quantlix deployment with server-side secrets. 

<script
  src="https://cdn.example.com/quantlix-assistant-widget.js"
  data-quantlix-assistant
  data-backend-url="https://assistant.example.com/chat"
  data-title="Quantlix Assistant"
  data-show-enforcement="true"></script>
